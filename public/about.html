About Equity Dental Partners

Equity Dental Partners is a leading independent DSO company, focused on insights, innovation and accelerating the transformation of its partnersthrough the power of its Platform.

 

Inspiring a Better Dental Care System

Our interconnected position at the center of healthcare allows us to serve as a catalyst for greater collaboration on the path to value-based and consumer-centric care. We are an inseparable part of today’s healthcare ecosystem—playing a vital role in helping the system to not only function, but to also work smarter.

A Key Catalyst of a Value-Based Healthcare System

 

We work alongside our customers and partners to accelerate the journey toward improved lives and healthier communities. 

How We Help

 

Comprehensive Software and Services for Healthcare Payers and Providers

We collaborate with dentists to apply innovative data and analytics solutions and services to help improve clinical decision-making; simplify billing, collection, and payment processes; and enable a better patient experience.

 

Addressing healthcare’s biggest challenges

We’re creating a stronger and more efficient healthcare system that enables better patient care, choice, and outcomes with a comprehensive suite of software, analytics, technology-enabled services, and network solutions for payers and providers. 

What We Do

Practical innovations and integrated solutions that solve problems at scale

We invest broadly in healthcare technology innovations that improve our products, speed healthcare industry transformation, and help our customers flourish.

Our Innovations

Values at the Core of Our Culture

Our core values are Pursue Purpose, Be Agile, Champion Innovation, Include All, and Earn Trust. These are the guideposts for our culture and drive our interactions internally as well as with our customers.
