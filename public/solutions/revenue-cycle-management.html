Software and Services Across the Revenue Cycle

We partner with provider organizations to prevent denials, accelerate reimbursement, and improve the patient experience. As your single trusted source for revenue cycle management, we help you drive efficiencies via disruptive technologies, exception-based workflows, and powerful platform connectivity. 

Front-End

Patient access, retail-style shopping, financial clearance, coverage, and price transparency solutions and services

Establish strength early in the revenue cycle, deliver a better patient experience, automate pre-authorization, avoid downstream denials, and move self-pay collections to the front of the revenue cycle.

Change Healthcare and Epic: Integration to Optimize Revenue Performance

Electronic health record (EHR) implementations can be disruptive and pose risk to many areas of hospital operations–– especially in the revenue cycle. Change Healthcare and Epic have a well-defined collaboration, designed to maximize the functionality of our solutions. Customers with Epic enjoy an improved end-user experience, increased productivity, and optimized reimbursement. Our patient access and analytics driven claims management solutions are optimized to complement Epic to support a better clinical and financial experience for providers and their patients.
